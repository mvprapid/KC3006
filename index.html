Cyber Security

What is Cyber Security

Cyber Security is the defence of computers and data from cyberattacks. These attacks aim to gain access to or disrupt the functionality of data and systems.

What is Encryption

Encryption is the scrambling of data to obscure it to prevent anyone without authorisation from reading it. the encrypted data appears random until the key is used to unscramble it. it can also be used maliciously to lock someone out of their data or computer to demand a ransom from them.

What is Malware

Malware is short for Malicious software and describes software that is harmful to a computer system or grants unauthorised access to a data. Malware is used to either stop the victim from using the system in their desired way, or to make money from them.

Computing Laws and Legislation

Computing laws and legislation control how computers can be used and the way in which data and Communications are handled.
